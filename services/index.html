ALIGNMENT SERVICE

Come to Mike’s High Torque Auto Performance to rotate tires and receive a wheel alignment service all in a single appointment. We are car alignment specialists in American Fork, UT offering 2-wheel and 4-wheel alignment service for front and back wheels.

If you notice your steering wheeling pulling more to the left or right, feel the vehicle vibrating when turning, or observe uneven tire tread, stop by for a car alignment inspection. Let us keep your vehicle safer on the road. Contact our alignment shop today!

CUSTOM EXHAUST

Visit our auto repair shop for muffler and custom exhaust services in American Fork, UT. A custom car exhaust system does more than just muffle the noise of your engine. It also redirects fumes away from the car’s cabin while lowering toxic gas emissions that lead to greater pollution.

No matter the make or model of your vehicle, our custom muffler shop can repair common car exhaust system problems including loud noises, vibrations when idling, and rust. Visit us in American Fork today to receive an estimate for your custom exhaust.

BRAKE REPAIR

Poorly working brakes can cause a world of problems for your safety and the safety of other drivers. Over time, brake pads begin to wear thin from years of friction. Once these wear out, it could affect the efficiency of other components to your vehicle’s brake system.

Don’t wait until your brakes are beyond repair! Visit Mike’s High Torque Auto Performance in American Fork, UT when you notice screeching or less resistance when pushing the brake pedal. Our mechanics will present you with the most affordable solution to the problem after completing an in-depth inspection.

STEERING & SUSPENSION

With all the weight of a vehicle resting on its suspension, these struts and shocks can experience a lot of wear and tear. Most mechanics recommend having steering and suspension inspected once every year to check how effective these systems are working and what can be done to fix them where needed.

As part of our steering and suspension inspection, we may need to perform a steering wheel alignment if you are having trouble keeping control. Along with an alignment service, struts may need to be replaced to absorb movement better, especially over bumpy roads. Bring your vehicle into our suspension and steering wheel alignment shop for an inspection today!
